About Pioneer Hackfeast

Pioneer Hackfeast is Birgunj’s flagship cybersecurity meetup bringing students, professionals and hobbyists together for hands-on learning, competitions and networking. Expect workshops, a CTF track, Red vs Blue demos, and lightning mentorship sessions.

  • Beginner-friendly workshops
  • Practical cyber labs
  • Community networking
  • Trophies & certificates
Highlights from last year's events — workshops, speaker sessions & CTF.

What you'll get

A jam-packed day of learning, competition, mentoring and prizes.
